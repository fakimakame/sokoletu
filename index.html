PROBLEM STATEMENT

The major problem that the society of Tanzania faces is knowing the price of products located in different local markets.

it causes the following problems

  • Understanding of agricultural product availability
  • Comparison of product prices from different markets
  • Uncertainty of availability of products in markets

Implementation

Intensive research was conducted through available Government resources with association of market monitoring.

The carried solution for implementation was creating a Mobile app and encourage on use of billboards. Hence the objectives of the team.
The to be designed informative platform was named “SOKO LETU”


What the platform actually do.
  1. Informs on agriculture product prices as vary in local markets
  2. The estimated quantity of products available.
  3. Agricultural warehouse available with its stock quantity.
  4. Statistics (i.e. Seasonal consumption, demand and supply).
  5. Export and imports report.
  6. Government policies and resources on agriculture.
  7. Connects on wholesales available.
